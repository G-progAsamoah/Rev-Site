I'm

Creative, Author, Artist, Lecturer

My work's final output is just the start of a new phase.

My works are inspired by social identities, human connections and culture. I question labels and stereotypes by re-presenting them in different ways to provoke critical thinking. The end product of my work is only the beginning of a new process. My interest is deeply rooted in material culture and conceptual clues which sustains and links the beginning of one idea to another. I seek engagements and create an eternity of musings.

Personal Philosophy

Engages deeply with spontaneous interactions across materials, spaces, and emotions, fostering impactful social connections. Art presents transformative visions for community growth.

Artist Identity

known as Kizitotheartist, is a Ghanaian creative shaping narratives through his art. His work bridges art, teaching, and literature.

Professional Roles

Accomplished artist, lecturer, author, and creative consultant with a passion for community building. His expertise spans creative expression, consultancy, and education.

Academic Background

Holds degrees in Fine Art, Management, Administration, and Marketing Research from prestigious Ghanaian institutions. His studies enrich his multidimensional artistic approach.

Recognition

Kizito is an internationally acclaimed artist celebrated for his cultural impact and award-winning works. His art connects local themes to a global audience.

Exhibition Highlights (2005-2023)

A globally recognized portfolio showcasing cultural identity, societal perspectives, and evolving African art across continents.
